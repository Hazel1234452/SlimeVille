Our Story 📖

how SlimeVille came to be

🧪💚🍓

SlimeVille started in a Dubai kitchen with one slightly-too-sticky batch of butter slime and a big dream. What began as weekend experiments — testing scents, chasing the perfect stretch, and covering the counter in glitter (sorry, Mum) — slowly turned into something bigger.

Batch after batch, we perfected our recipes: the softest butter slimes, the fluffiest clouds, the crunchiest bead-filled jars. Friends tried them. Then friends of friends. Then people we'd never met started asking where they could buy one.

So we built SlimeVille — a place where every single jar is still hand-mixed, hand-scented, and hand-sprinkled with the same care as batch number one. No factories, no shortcuts. Just really, really good slime.

We're a small team with a simple mission: squish, stretch, repeat — and make Dubai a little more satisfying, one jar at a time. 💚

And SlimeVille just got bigger! We're now two co-founders — best friends who decided that making slime together beats making it alone. One of us handles the mixing, the labels and the shop; the other brings the designs, the ideas, and (coming this August 🎬) the videos. Same slime, same love, double the chaos. 💚💜

Questions? Answers! ❓

everything you're wondering

🛡️ Is the slime safe?
Yes! All our slimes are made with non-toxic, kid-friendly ingredients. That said — never eat slime (even the ones that smell like dessert 🍰), always wash hands after playing, and it's not suitable for children under 3.
🚗 Where do you deliver & how much?
We deliver anywhere in Dubai for a flat 5 AED delivery fee. After you order, we'll WhatsApp you within 24 hours to arrange your delivery day!
💳 How do I pay?
Two easy options: 💵 cash on delivery or 📱 bank transfer — you pick at checkout, and we'll confirm the details on WhatsApp.
⏳ How long does slime last?
With good care, 2-3 months of squishy freshness! Keep the jar sealed tight, play with clean hands, and store it away from heat and sunlight (yes, especially in Dubai summers 🥵). Every order comes with a free care guide card!
💧 Help — my slime got sticky!
Totally normal, especially in warm weather! Leave the jar open for 10 minutes and knead — it usually firms right up. Still sticky? Message us and we'll help you fix it 💚
🔄 Do you restock sold-out flavors?
Yes! We mix fresh batches every week. Join the Slime Club below 💌 and you'll be the first to know when your favourite is back (plus secret new flavor drops 👀).
